were almost destitute of beards, they never enjoyed

either the manly graces of youth, or the venerable

aspect of age. Against these enemies, Hermanric

prepared to exert the united forces of the Gothic

state: but he soon discovered that his vassal tribes,

provoked by oppression, were much more inclined to

second, than to repel, the invasion of the Huns.
